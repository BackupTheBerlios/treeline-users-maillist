Tomas Valusek wrote:
> Hello,
> 
> I'd like to format output of a tree entry like this:
> 
> Item 1:
> {item1}
> 
> item 2:
> {item2}
> 
> But in output view/printout, I get following:
> 
> Item 1:
> {item1}
> item 2:
> {item2}
> 
> Note the missing empty line between {item1{ and the literal text "Item 2".
> 
> Is there any way to do this? All items use plain text format with 
> preserved line breaks.

Try going to "Tools->File Options" and check the boxes for "Add blank
lines between nodes" and "Add line breaks afer each line".
