If you mean enter the date in a date field, it's already there.  Each
date field in the Data Editor pane has a pull-down menu with options for
"today", "yesterday" and "tomorrow".  Or you can set the "Default Value
for New Nodes" (in the "Field Config" tab of the "Configure Data Types"
dialog) to "Now", and the date field will be automatically populated
with the creation date of the node.

Or did you mean something else?
