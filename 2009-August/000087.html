On the other hand, here is a plug-in I wrote a while ago that, in addition
to a couple of other things, allows you to insert the date or time into any
editable field (where the cursor is) at any time. It uses alt-N for
inserting the date (yyyy-mm-dd format) and alt-Q for inserting the time (24
hour format).
