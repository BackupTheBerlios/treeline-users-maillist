John Gilbrough wrote:
> Doug -
> 
> I installed the latest and everything works as advertised, and now
> there's find for the help - thank you.
> 
> A downside of upgrading was that I lost my treeview configuration
> settings.  I've got them all back to where they were except there is
> one that I cannot find.  So what I am looking for is how one leaves
> the nodes off the right hand view.  Where would that be set?

Leaves which nodes off of the right-hand view?  To leave off children,
toggle  Tools > General Options > Startup Condition > Show children in
right-hand view.

To leave off the parent node, you can try sliding the view pane
divider all the way up to give the parent protion of the view a zero
height, but it might not stay set like that.
